Bergamo's History

An immersive path in the historical part of the city of Bergamo. The itinerary starts from the suggestive funicular that will bring you almost in the center of Bergamo Alta. You will then pass through Piazza vecchia an admire all the beautiful monuments inside it: Campanone and S.Maria maggiore. Finally, after passing near to Palazzo Terzi, you will find the well known Mura Veneziane that will accompany you to Porta S.Giacomo

Duration: 1:30 h

You will go through the following places

Funicolare città alta
Piazza vecchia
S.Maria Maggiore
Campanone
Palazzo terzi
Mura Veneziane
Porta S.Giacomo

Vision

The vision of Locus is to provide an immersive and wonderful experience in visiting one of the most beautiful city of northern Italy: Bergamo. The history of Bergamo is full of art, nature and adventures and our vision is to spread it all over the word making it accesible with our intercative website!
