Churches and life

An immersive path in the historical part of the city of Bergamo. The itinerary starts from the immersive Piazza Vecchia at the center of Città Alta. You will then pass through the near S.Maria and S.Alessandro Cathedrals. Going on to the Antico lavatoio and Ex. Chiesa S.Rocco, you will then reach the first historically asphalted road of the city: via Solata. Finally, the itinerary terminates in the Convento of S.Francesco.

Duration: 1:45 h

You will go through the following places

Piazza vecchia
S.Maria Maggiore
Cattedrale S.Alessandro
Antico lavatoio
Ex Chiesa S.Rocco
Via Solata
Convento di S.Francesco
Bergamo, Lombardia (IT)

Vision
