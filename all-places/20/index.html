Museo delle Scienze

The Enrico Caffi Museum of Natural Sciences in Bergamo, located in Città Alta, holds collections referring to all naturalistic disciplines (more than one million of artifacts), and has an exhibition area about 1,800 m². The museum was founded in 1871, and officially opened in 1918.
