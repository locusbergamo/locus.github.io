S.Agostino in summer

The weekend of the third week of July, during the evenings of Saturday and Sunday, in Piazza Sant’Agostino, a typical local festival will take place. During the evenings, it will be possible to meet friends, and have some funny, everything will be accompanied by numerous street food stalls, live music and much more.

Piazzale S.Agostino

The Piazza of Sant’Agostino owes its name to the church dedicated to the saint of the same name, which overlooks the square. Historical sources report that this church was already present in the area during the XIV century and was later deconsecrated in 1797 with the Napoleonic invasion.
